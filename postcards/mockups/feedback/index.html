Feedback for Natalie Snyder

  1. Out of the three design explorations, the most successful one is the middle, neon yellow design. I really enjoy the cutout aestheic as it appears extremely graphic and eyecatching. I would encourage you to continue to use neon for your color pallette as it seems to evoke a clean and modern tone which I believe fits the quotes well.
  2. I would be careful with creating hyphens and line-breaks. Unless it's neccecary for the composition, you should be able to avoid most hyphenation.
  3. Your cards may benefit from a typeface with a larger x-height for easier readability or use a larger font size.
